About Tupi

Tupi is:

  • A design and authoring tool for digital artists interested in 2D Animation. It Offers an interface experience focused on kids from 8-100 years old
  • A software application developed in C++ using the Qt library
  • A free software project under constant redesign and evolution, released under the GPL terms (version 3)
  • A community of artists and developers around a software project
  • A space for creating and publishing free animated content

The name of the project is inspired by the Tupi people, one of the main ethnic groups of Brazilian indigenous people, along with the Guarani natives with which they share a strong relationship.

For more information about Tupi please visit: http://www.maefloresta.com
